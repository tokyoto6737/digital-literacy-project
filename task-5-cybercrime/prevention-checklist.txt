Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Verify the identity of the person before making payments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Use strong passwords for banking apps
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Use trusted and secure payment apps only

Financial Safety Tips:
- Remember that entering UPI PIN means money is being sent
- Do not accept “collect requests” from unknown sources

Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
